Steps :
  • Find an image in Google like "dog playing" or "people walking" or "baby swimming" or "trekking" or "rock climbing" and copy the link of the Image, it should specifically be image link and not the website link
    In Mobile: Tap on the Google image for 2 seconds and select open image in new tab and then copy the link from there.
    In Desktop: Right click on the google image and select copy image link.
  • Paste the link in the text box and click on submit, wait for a few seconds it will generate a caption and show u.
